Prúndera

Prúndera is Erdáin's only moon. It orbits around the same distance that the Earth's moon does, but only 62% of its mass. With its smaller size, it is only around 76% in size and 80% in brightness compared to the Earth's moon in the sky. Because of this, solar eclipses with Narháera are impossible, but they are definetly possible with Ghaláera. This smaller gravitational force that Prúndera has on Erdáin also helps in explaining the planet's large axial tilt, and help compensating its strong ocean currents with shorter tides.
