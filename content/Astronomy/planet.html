The Planet

Erdain is in a rocky planet that has many features very different to Earth. It rotates around a Main Sequence Star, although unlike earth, it finds itself in a Type-S Binary Star System. The presence of this other star have marginally but observable effects in the planet's climate and biology. Despite being very distant, Ghaláera is twice as big as the star that Erdain orbits, and it is very radioactive.
The other main difference is with Erdain's high obliquity that largely modifies the planet's climate, as will be discussed later.
